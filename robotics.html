My Journey and Achievements

Singapore Vex competitions

I have over 2 years of Vex experience and have particiapted in a multitude of different competitions.

My experience

Vex competitions were always extremely challenging and competitive, requiring many hours of preperation. Many hours had to be sacrificed after school to prepare for competitions.

However, overall, Vex was still extremely fun and an eye opening experience that allowed me to discover my passion and love for applied science in the form of engineering.

My contributions

I was appointed the CCA commitee head of Vex, which put me in charge of orgnaising all things to do with Vex, namely the training of the Juniors.

Without any prior mentors due to our school stepping away from Vex competitions during the Covid-19 pandemic, I had to come up with creative ways to equip our juniors with the skills needed in order to succeed in the harsh and competitive environement of Vex competitions.
