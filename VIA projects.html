Digital Citizenship Leader

In Secondary 3, I joined the newly established Digital Citizenship board where our mission was to promote the safe use of Technology in our school, bringing awareness to issues such as cyberbullying and the use of Artificial Intelligence in school work.

Digitial Citizenship Outreach

The Digital Citizenship board organised many Outreaches where we would go to different primary schools across Singapore to educate the students about safe internet use during a half-day camp, where I participated as a faciliator.

House leader

I also joined our school's House Leadership committee where I had the chance to be a facilitator on our schools annual Outdoor Exploration Expedition where the Secondary 2 students would embark on a 20km hike through Bukit Timah Hill.

Girls in Stem

The Robotics@Apex CCA also organised a Girls in Stem outreach program where we invited girls from many primary schools across Singapore to participate in a half-day program where they learn to use the Stick 'em Robotics system to complete challenges.

Class service learning

Our class participated in many service learning projects. This year in particular, we helped out with the logistics at Willing Hearts.
